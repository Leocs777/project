# TMS Labs Brand Direction

## Selected Direction

The current website uses a simple, mature TMS Labs identity inspired by the preferred Canva option A:

- Clear `TMS Labs` wordmark.
- Deep forest green square mark.
- Clear `TMS` monogram for better recognition at navigation size.
- Warm gold development line that suggests project growth and construction progress.
- Minimal structure suitable for website navigation, favicon, business cards, and deployment branding.
